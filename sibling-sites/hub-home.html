Overview

Features

  • Hub and News site names with a symbiotic relationship
  • Search that operates across all 24 journals
  • Profile linked to WOL but not to the news site
  • List of all journals
  • Topic links that would point to manually curated topic pages
  • Other publication links that would point to the magazines on the news site
  • A link to the news site
  • Featured content from one of the 24 journals
  • Fixture that points users to the news site
  • List of the 24 journals
  • Manually curated 'Featured' content that does not have to rely on digital objects and could point to anywhere
  • Article feed rendering content from the 24 journals
  • Right rail news feed bringing content in from the news site

Risks

  • Name change on WAS may not be possible
  • Any integration based on feeding content between sites could have significant technical dependencies
    • We can fake some of this by having custom HTML widgets render content, but this will need to be edited and updated manually.
  • Concept of subject pages based on WAS taxonomy on hub brings its own risks (see subject page for breakdown)
  • If a subject has no affiliated content on hub, but does on the news site, do we still show the subject link?
  • Some journals may need to cross multiple T1s (do you repeat journals?)
